Bio

I received my undergraduate degree in Policy Analysis, an interdisciplinary degree covering economics and other social sciences. I gravitate towards intersections because that’s where the nuance of life is best represented in my eyes.

One of those intersections is between civic engagement and technology. After spending two years in Peace Corps Guatemala I knew I had to be more technical to start offering solutions to age old problems of power and privilege. Tech needed me and I needed it.

Now I’m building the knowledge to be a full-stack developer using Ruby on Rails, JavaScript, and CSS/HTML. I also focus on blockchain technologies for civic engagement. Check out my portfolio page for more information.
